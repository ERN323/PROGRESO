One Rep Max (1RM)

Your 1-Rep Max is the maximum amount of weight you could theoretically lift for a single, full repetition of an exercise.

Testing your actual 1RM can be dangerous. Instead, PROGRESO calculates an estimate using the standard Epley Formula:

1RM ≈ Weight × (1 + Reps / 30)

* Note: This is an approximate mathematical calculation. Its accuracy depends on your repetition ranges (best calculated for sets of 10 reps or fewer).
